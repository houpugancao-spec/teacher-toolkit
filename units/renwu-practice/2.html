任务二｜根据解释写孔子名言

Read the Chinese and English meanings, then write the original saying.

名言 1
只学习不思考，会迷惑；只思考不学习，会陷入困境。
Learning without thinking leads to confusion; thinking without learning leads to trouble.
名言 2
几个人同行，一定有能当我老师的。学习他们的长处，改正自己的短处。
Among any three people, one can be my teacher—follow their strengths and correct my own faults.
名言 3
知道就说知道，不知道就说不知道，这才是真正的智慧。
To know what you know, and admit what you do not know—that is real wisdom.
名言 4
不断学习，永不感到满足。
Never tire of learning.
